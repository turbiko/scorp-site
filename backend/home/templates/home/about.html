About us

Animagrad studio was founded in 2012 as a part of FILM.UA Group and has become one of the leading animation studios in Eastern Europe. Our portfolio lists a number of successful products in 2D and 3D.

Animagrad’s mission is about bringing Ukrainian animation into a new age of excellence. Our professional team of highly trained experts in 2D and 3D animation, creative department and skilled managers successfully implements original projects and supplies highly qualified services for accomplishing various tasks in animation production.

We aspire towards the highest international standards of both creative and technical work.

Currently Animagrad produces and develops several animated features and series intended for the international market.
